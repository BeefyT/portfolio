    Chest Workout Form

    Bench Press

    Here are some tips:

  • Grip the bar wide, slightly beyond shoulder width
  • lay down and bring your legs back towards your torso as far as you can with your feet flat on the ground. This allows your back muscles to tighten allowing you to bench more
  • Arch your back and tighten your back muscles to help use your back muscles in this lift
  • Lift the bar off the rack, take a deep breath filling your stomach, this will tighten your corea allowing you to push with more force
  • Squeeze the bar this will allow you to push more

    Back Workout Form

    Deadlift

    Here are some tips:

  • Grip the bar wide, slightly beyond shoulder width
  • Feet should be about shoulder width apart
  • Grip the bar in an over under grip. One hand with palm over the bar, one hand palm under the bar
  • When lifitng keep the bar close to your body to avoid lower back stress
  • Take a deep breath into stomach to tighten core and protect spine

  • Take a deep breath into stomach to tighten core and protect spine
  • Make sure to not over extend your arm down.

    Check Your Form

    Back Squats

    Here are some tips:

  • Legs should be slightly wider then shoulders
  • Place the bar on top of your trap muscles
  • Take a deep breath to tighten core and protect spine before squating
  • Toes should feel like they are screwing into the ground
  • Keep back straight and squat to a 90 degree angle
  • If difficult to reach correct depth, grab a box to sit on

    • Front Squats

    Here are some tips:

  • Take a deep breath into stomach to tighten core and protect spine
  • Place bar on your upper chest muscles and criss cross arms over bar if wrists aren't flexible enough

    • Romanian Deadlifts

    Here are some tips:

  • Grip the bar wide, slightly beyond shoulder width
  • Keep legs straight and about shoulder width apat
  • Take a deep breath to tighten core and protect your spine
